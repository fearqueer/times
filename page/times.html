How to Use

  • Enter your clock-in and clock-out times in the entry fields.
  • You can enter a colon (:), period (.), or a comma (,) to separate the hour and minutes of the times.
  • Please note that times should be between 7:00 and 6:00. This limitation will be removed in the next update.
  • Currently, overtime is not calculated, but it is coming soon!
